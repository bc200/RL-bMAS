# OASIS Argumentation Research Trace

This ARA folder records research decisions, evidence, and pivots for the OASIS
argumentation-layer experiments.

## Layer Index

- `logic/problem.md`: current research problem and task fit.
- `logic/claims.md`: falsifiable claims from experiments and analysis.
- `logic/solution/heuristics.md`: reusable design heuristics.
- `trace/exploration_tree.yaml`: research path and pivots.
- `trace/sessions/`: session records.
- `evidence/README.md`: evidence artifacts and local result files.
- `staging/observations.yaml`: unpromoted observations.
